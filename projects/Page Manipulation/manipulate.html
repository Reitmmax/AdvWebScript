Did You Know?

The Top Selling Game is Minecraft

Minecraft is the top selling game with 300mil sales, followed by Grand Theft Auto V with 190mil sales, and then Tetris with 100mil sales.

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_best-selling_video_games

The Best Selling Game Series is Mario

Mario has a total of 823mil+ sales, followed by Tetris with 495mil sales, then Pokemon with 480mil

https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_best-selling_video_game_franchises

The First Game Ever Made Was Tennis for Two

Created by Physicist William Higinbotham in October of 1958! The game is a simple tennis game, similiar to Pong. Players would used knobs to adjust the angles of the ball and a button to hit the ball to the other player

https://www.aps.org/publications/apsnews/200810/physicshistory.cfm#:~:text=Higinbotham%20made%20some%20drawings%2C%20and,the%20game%20Tennis%20for%20Two.

The Best Selling Console is the PlayStation 2

The PlayStation had 158mil sales, followed by the Nintendo DS with 154mil sales, then the Nintendo Switch 131mil sales.
